Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ForexMastery. Can't find your answer? Chat with our team.

Do I need any prior trading experience?
+
Absolutely not. Our Forex Fundamentals course starts from zero — assuming no prior knowledge. We guide you step-by-step through every concept before advancing. Thousands of our students had never seen a trading chart before joining.
How much capital do I need to start trading?
+
We recommend starting with a demo account (no real money) for at least 2–3 months before going live. When you're ready, you can start with as little as $100–$500. Our risk management courses will teach you how to grow any account size responsibly.
Are the live signals guaranteed to be profitable?
+
No signal service can guarantee profits — trading involves risk. Our signals are generated by experienced traders with a documented 94% win rate, but past performance doesn't guarantee future results. We always include stop losses and teach you proper risk management.
Can I cancel my subscription at any time?
+
Yes, absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time with zero penalties. Your access continues until the end of your current billing period. We also offer a 7-day free trial on all plans so you can experience the platform before committing.
How long do I have access to the course content?
+
As long as your subscription is active, you have full unlimited access to all course content including any new lessons we add. Elite plan members receive lifetime access to all content even after cancellation.
What markets are covered — only Forex?
+
While our primary focus is the Forex market, the strategies and concepts we teach apply to Indices, Gold, Oil, and Commodities as well. Many of our students successfully trade multiple markets using the skills they learn here.
Is there a refund policy?

Yes. If you're not satisfied within the first 30 days of a paid subscription, we offer a full money-back guarantee — no questions asked. We're confident in our product and want you to be 100% happy.
